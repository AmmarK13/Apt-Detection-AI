**Smoke Testing** is a quick, surface-level test to verify that the major components of your software pipeline work as expected â€” it's like turning on a machine to see if it powers up, before diving into detailed tests.

In the context of **ML/AI model deployment**, **smoke testing ensures that**:

- The model is loadable.
- The data can be read.
- The transformation works.
- Predictions can be made.
- Predictions are valid.
